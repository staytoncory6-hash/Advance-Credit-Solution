Common Questions

Can you guarantee results?

No. Nobody can honestly guarantee specific results or score jumps. What we do is focus on real errors, missing info, and outdated items that the law says must be accurate and verifiable.

Is this legal?

Yes, you have the legal right to dispute inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated information on your credit reports. We never tell you to lie, create fake documents, or dispute valid debt just to “see what happens.”

What does the AI actually do?

Elite AI gives you explanations, ideas, and educational guidance based on your questions. It is not a lawyer or financial advisor, and it doesn’t send letters or log in to your accounts.

How do I pay?

For paid services like the Snapshot Plan, you’ll use Cash App as explained during your onboarding. We keep it simple and transparent.
