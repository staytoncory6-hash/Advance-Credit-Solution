How the Rounds System Works

Think of this like a calm, structured fight plan. No rage. No nonsense. Just a sequence that moves from “asking nicely” to “bringing in regulators” if they refuse to follow the law.

Round 1 – Clean Initial Disputes

Based on your Snapshot and story, Elite AI helps you shape your first round of letters to the bureaus. The goal: correct, incomplete, or unverified data – no fluff, no fake laws.

Round 2 – Follow-Up Pressure

If some items come back “verified” or ignored, Round 2 goes a level deeper. More detail, more clarity, and a stronger reminder of your rights under the FCRA.

Round 3 – Method of Verification

Here we start asking how they verified, what procedures they used, and why obviously bad data is still being reported. This round is about the process, not just the result.

Round 4 – Regulator Escalation

If bureaus or furnishers keep stonewalling, it may be time to bring in outside eyes: CFPB, FTC, state Attorney General, and other regulators. This is escalation, not anger.
