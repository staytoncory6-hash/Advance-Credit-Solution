Monthly Service Options

Starter Clean-Up

Ideal if you have a handful of collections, some late payments, and outdated personal info, but not a complete disaster.

  • Focus: Inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated negative items.
  • Clean-up of names, addresses, and employers that don’t belong.
  • Structured dispute cycles with tracking and mail reminders.
  • Basic rebuilding guidance so new positive history starts working for you.

Best for: Light–moderate damage files.

Elite Restoration

For serious clean-up: multiple collections, charge-offs, public records, or identity theft.

  • Focus: Deep restoration and long-term stability.
  • Advanced letters: incomplete info, returned mail, reinsertion, and more.
  • Help organizing documentation (police reports, ID theft proof, statements).
  • Full-year roadmap: clean-up plus rebuilding with trade lines and rent reporting.

Best for: Heavy damage, big future goals.

Education + DIY Assist

For people who want to stay hands-on, but still want guidance, letters, and the digital book.

  • Includes the A.I. Credit Survival Guide digital book.
  • Letter templates you can reuse forever, plus AI help rewording them.
  • Checkpoints so you’re not stuck wondering what to do next.
  • Great for long-term “credit maintenance” and self-education.

Best for: DIY learners and budget-conscious clients.
