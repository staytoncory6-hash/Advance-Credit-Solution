Credit Cleanup Checklist

Use this checklist as a roadmap before sending any disputes.

  1. Pull all three credit reports (Equifax, Experian, TransUnion).
  2. Highlight every negative item: collections, charge-offs, lates, etc.
  3. Write the creditor name, balance, and dates for each item.
  4. Mark anything suspicious or inaccurate.
  5. Group similar issues together.
  6. Choose the easiest or most inaccurate items to dispute first.
  7. Create a folder for letters, reports, and responses.
  8. Track mailing dates and deadlines.
